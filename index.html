SPIDER-MAN

When teenage Peter Parker is bitten by a radioactive spider, he gains incredible superhuman abilities including wall-crawling, web-slinging, and enhanced strength. As he learns to master his newfound powers, Peter must embrace his destiny as Spider-Man to protect New York City from the dangerous Green Goblin, while balancing his secret identity with his responsibilities as a student and his relationships with those he loves most.
